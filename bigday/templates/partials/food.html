Food

Here are some places you can grab a bite to eat! Food will be served at the wedding.

Arni's

This was a favorite of Allison and her sisters growing up. The garlic bread a.k.a. Texas toast was always her favorite.

Pizza King

An Indiana classic. If you're keen enough to eat here in person you can get your drinks delivered to you on a train.

Bruno's Pizza

This is definitely the best pizza in the area. A Purdue classic and a frequently requested meal by Allison and her sisters whenever they are in town.

Shellie's Cafe

This is a special one because Allison's favorite Aunt Janet used to take them here for breakfast. It's also conveniently across the street from our wedding venue!

Mary Lou Donuts

If you're looking for some great local donuts look no further than Mary Lou's. Be sure to try a bear claw!

Chipotle

No matter where you go, Chipotle will always be there. Fun facts about Chipotle, Allison's senior year she ate at the local Hamden Chipotle almost daily. Feel free to join in our game of "Over/Under": do you think within your lifetime you have eaten Chipotle over or under 750 times?

Outback Steakhouse

This will forever be Lance's favorite. Our place in Arizona was right next to one that Lance would frequent. If you want to get his favorite meal be sure to get the full rack of ribs.

Triple XXX

While we've never eaten here, it was featured on the very famous Guy Fierri's "Diners, Drive-ins, and Dives."

Dog N Suds

If you want a good 'ol drive-in restaurant, Dog N Suds is the place to be. Be sure to try a root beer float!

Panera

Lance truthfully despises Panera, but it's still an easy stop and not too far from the recommended hotels!

Dairy Queen

When Allison and her family would come to spend summers in West Lafayette they would go here for a late night treat! Be sure to try a blizzard.

La Bamba

Here, you can get a burrito as big as your face!

There are various places to eat near the venue.
