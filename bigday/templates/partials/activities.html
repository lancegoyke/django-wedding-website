Activities

If you find yourself with extra free time, here are some activities you could do in the area.

Hiking

Lafayette has a few flat hikes.

Art Museum

The Haan Museum of Indiana Art has paintings, ceramics, and anitique American furniture.

Nature Center

The Lilly Nature Center consists of award-winning exhibits and activities designed for the entire family. The bird viewing area has one-way glass so visitors can see the birds at the feeding stations up close. This is located inside the Celery Bog Nature Area.

Celery Bog

The Celery Bog Nature Area is approximately 195 acres, consisting of a prairie, a savanna, woodlands and wetlands. Water Park

Tropicanoe Cove is a public swimming pool with a small admission fee.

Fort Ouiatenon

Fort Ouiatenon is a replica of the wooden blockhouse that was originally built here in 1717 by the French.

Escape Room

Mission: Breakout Lafayette gives your group one hour to figure out an adventure puzzle.

Bowling

Lance's old hockey friend has a bowling alley up near the fairgrounds: Mike Aulby's Arrowhead Bowl.

Eat

There are various places to eat near the venue.

Shopping

There are various shopping centers in Lafayette.
